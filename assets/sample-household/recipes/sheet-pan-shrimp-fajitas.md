## Ingredients
- 1.5 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 2 bell peppers, sliced
- 1 red onion, sliced
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 teaspoons chili powder
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- juice of 1 lime

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
2. Toss vegetables with 1 tablespoon oil and half the spices; roast 12 minutes.
3. Toss shrimp with the remaining oil, spices, and lime juice.
4. Add shrimp to the sheet pan and roast 6-8 more minutes until shrimp are opaque.
5. Serve with tortillas or over greens.
