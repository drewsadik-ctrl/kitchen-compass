## Ingredients

### Burger Meat
- 3 pounds ground beef
- 3 teaspoons onion powder
- 1 1/2 teaspoons dried oregano
- 3 teaspoons garlic powder
- 3 teaspoons paprika
- 6 teaspoons salt
- 1 1/2 teaspoons black pepper

### Salad / Bowl Base
- romaine lettuce
- tomatoes
- pickles
- red onion
- 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
- avocado, if available (optional)

### Special Sauce
- 3/4 cup mayo
- 3 tablespoons ketchup
- 3 teaspoons yellow mustard
- 3 tablespoons pickle juice
- dill relish, optional

## Instructions
1. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Add the ground beef and all burger meat seasonings.
3. Brown the beef, breaking it up as it cooks, until fully cooked through.
4. While the beef is browning, make the special sauce by whisking together the mayo, ketchup, yellow mustard, pickle juice, and dill relish if using.
5. Assemble the bowls. Start with romaine lettuce, then add the toppings and cooked ground beef.
6. Drizzle the special sauce over everything and serve.
