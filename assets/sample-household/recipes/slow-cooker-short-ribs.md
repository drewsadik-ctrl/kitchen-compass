## Ingredients
- 4 lb bone-in beef short ribs
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 yellow onion, chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, smashed
- 2 tablespoons tomato paste
- 1 cup dry red wine
- 2 cups beef stock
- 2 sprigs rosemary
- salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Season ribs with salt and pepper; sear in olive oil on all sides, about 2 minutes per side.
2. Transfer ribs to the slow cooker.
3. Sauté onions in the same pan until soft; add garlic and tomato paste, cook 1 minute.
4. Deglaze with wine, scrape the fond, simmer 3 minutes.
5. Pour pan mixture, stock, and rosemary into the slow cooker.
6. Cook on low 8 hours until ribs are fork-tender.
7. Skim fat and serve over mashed potatoes with pan sauce.
