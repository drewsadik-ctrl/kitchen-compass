## Ingredients
- 2 heads broccoli, cut into florets
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- black pepper
- optional: lemon wedges for serving

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
2. Toss broccoli with olive oil, salt, and pepper on a rimmed sheet pan.
3. Roast 15-18 minutes until edges are browned.
4. Serve with lemon if using.
