## Ingredients
- 2 lb russet or yukon gold potatoes, peeled and chunked
- 4 cloves garlic, smashed
- 3/4 cup whole milk, warmed
- 4 tablespoons butter
- salt and pepper

## Instructions
1. Boil potatoes and garlic in salted water until fork-tender, about 15 minutes.
2. Drain and return to the pot over low heat for 1 minute to dry out.
3. Mash with butter, then stir in warm milk a splash at a time.
4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
