## Ingredients
- 4 pounds boneless pork shoulder (pork butt)
- 1 tablespoon kosher salt (or 2 teaspoons table salt)
- black pepper, to taste
- sazon seasoning, optional
- BBQ sauce, optional for serving
- buns, optional for serving

## Instructions
1. Season the pork all over with salt, black pepper, and any additional seasoning you want to use.
2. Place the pork in the slow cooker.
3. Cover and cook on low for about 8 hours, until the internal temperature reaches at least 200°F and the meat shreds easily.
4. Remove the pork to a board and let it rest for at least 20 minutes, ideally 1 hour.
5. Shred the pork with two forks.
6. Strain the liquid from the slow cooker to remove solids.
7. If desired, chill the liquid briefly and remove excess fat.
8. Pour the strained juices back over the shredded pork and toss.
9. Serve as is, or toss with BBQ sauce.
